<p>Tika - Content Analysis Toolkit
</p>
<p>Apache Tika is a toolkit for detecting and extracting metadata and structured text content 
</p>
<p>from various documents using existing parser libraries. 
</p>
<p>Apache Tika is an effort undergoing incubation at The Apache Software Foundation (ASF), 
</p>
<p>sponsored by the Apache Lucene PMC. Incubation is required of all newly accepted projects 
</p>
<p>until a further review indicates that the infrastructure, communications, and decision making 
</p>
<p>process have stabilized in a manner consistent with other successful ASF projects. While 
</p>
<p>incubation status is not necessarily a reflection of the completeness or stability of the code, it 
</p>
<p>does indicate that the project has yet to be fully endorsed by the ASF.
</p>
<p>See the Apache Tika Incubation Status page for the current incubation status.
</p>
<p>Latest News
</p>
<p>March 22nd, 2007: Apache Tika project started
</p>
<p>The Apache Tika project was formally started when the Tika proposal was accepted by the Apache 
</p>
<p>Incubator PMC. </p>
